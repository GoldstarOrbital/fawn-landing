Your deposits sit in an FDIC-insured account, fully liquid, fully yours. We do not invest, lend, or pool your money. You can withdraw at any time. FAWN’s only revenue comes from the per-transaction fee — not from using your balance for anything else. This is by design: your money is not our asset.
Every time you complete a transaction, FAWN charges $0.01–$0.02. That’s our entire revenue model. We also offer an optional $3/month Premium plan for higher transfer limits and custom features. We don’t charge monthly fees, we don’t sell your data, and we don’t lend your deposits. The business is profitable when you use the account — not when you forget about a fee.
No hard credit check to open an account. We verify your identity (as required by banking regulations) but we do not pull your credit score, and opening a FAWN account will not affect your credit. This is a checking account, not a loan product.
FAWN does not charge overdraft fees. If you don’t have sufficient funds for a transaction, the transaction will be declined — no penalty, no fee, no notification to your parents. We think a $35 penalty on a $5 mistake is predatory, so we eliminated it entirely.
