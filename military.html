Military & Veteran Savings

Your service is worth real discounts.

Verified discount programs for veteran students and ROTC cadets, plus local savings near every U.S. service academy. We tag who’s actually eligible for what — eligibility rules differ a lot between veteran, active duty, and ROTC status.

General Programs
Military, Veteran & ROTC Discounts

Most retail military discounts require ID.me verification as Active Duty, Veteran, or Reserve/Guard. ROTC cadets are not yet active duty, so eligibility varies by program — check each one before assuming it applies to you.

Service Academies
Academy Cadet Savings

Cadets live in barracks with most costs covered, so "cheapest housing" doesn't apply here. Instead: liberty-weekend spots, travel home, gear, and exchange/commissary savings near each academy.

Heads up: we don't list bars or alcohol deals for academy towns. Alcohol policy at every service academy is strict, class-year-dependent, and governed by your own chain of command — not something FAWN should be pointing you toward. Know your academy's regs.

FAWN doesn't control these third-party programs or academy-area businesses — terms, prices, and eligibility change without notice. Verify directly before relying on any of these. Know a program or local deal we're missing? Tell us.
