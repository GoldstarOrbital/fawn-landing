FAWN investor pitch sheet - source-backed draft

Student-first banking that turns fee pain into product loyalty.

FAWN is building a low-friction account experience for students and young adults: penny-per-transaction pricing, no overdraft penalty positioning, AI-personalized financial news, campus savings, military savings, and founding-member upside.
