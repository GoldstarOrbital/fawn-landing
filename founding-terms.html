Founding Member Terms

Updated June 19, 2026

Short version: You're paying upfront for early access and lifetime Premium. If FAWN never launches or you change your mind within 30 days, you get a full refund - no forms, no questions.

What you're buying

A Founding Member or Inner Circle purchase entitles you to:

  • Priority access to FAWN when it opens accounts to the public.
  • FAWN Premium features locked in at $0/month for the life of your account.
  • Founding member status visible in-app (badge + name in the credits roll).
  • Inner Circle only: a $25 account credit applied when your FAWN checking account opens, and the ability to vote on the product roadmap.

What you are NOT buying

This is not a deposit, not securities, and not equity in GoldstarOrbital Inc. It is a pre-purchase of digital access and perks. FAWN's banking services (deposits, debit cards, ACH) will be provided by an FDIC-insured partner bank through Unit at launch; your Founding Member purchase does not create an account with that bank until you complete the standard account-opening flow at launch.

Refund policy

You can request a full refund for any reason within 30 days of purchase. Email alex@getfawn.com with the email address you used at checkout and we'll process it via Stripe within 3 business days.

After 30 days, your Founding Member status is locked in for whenever FAWN launches - no expiration. If FAWN does not launch within 24 months of your purchase date, you are entitled to a full refund on request, regardless of how much time has passed.

Spot caps

Founding Member is capped at 20 total purchases. Inner Circle is capped at 5 total purchases. When a cap is reached, the corresponding Stripe payment link is deactivated and no further sales are accepted at that tier.

If FAWN doesn't launch

We are committed to building this. But "we'll try our best" is not a refund policy. If FAWN has not opened accounts to founding members within 24 months of your purchase, or if the project is discontinued before then, every founding member receives a full refund automatically - you don't need to ask.

Disputes

We'd rather solve any problem directly. Email alex@getfawn.com first. If we can't resolve it, disputes are governed by the laws of the State of California.
