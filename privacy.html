Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

FAWN ("we", "us", "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights. If you have questions, contact us at alexmarcusgoldsmith@gmail.com.

What data we collect

When you join the waitlist or create an account, we collect your first name and email address. When you open a bank account through our platform, additional identity verification information (full name, date of birth, address) is collected and processed by our banking partner, Unit, as required by federal Know Your Customer (KYC) regulations.

We also collect basic usage data (pages visited, interactions) to improve the product. We do not collect sensitive financial data beyond what is necessary to operate your account.

How we use your data

Your email and name are used to send waitlist updates, account notifications, and product announcements. We do not send unsolicited marketing to third parties on your behalf. You can unsubscribe from marketing emails at any time.

Identity information collected during account opening is used solely to satisfy regulatory requirements and is processed by Unit under their privacy policy.

Our banking partner: Unit

FAWN is a fintech platform, not a bank. Banking services - including deposit accounts and payment processing - are provided through Unit, a banking-as-a-service provider whose bank partners are FDIC members. Unit's privacy practices govern how your financial account data is handled. You can review Unit's privacy policy at unit.co/privacy.

Data storage and security

Waitlist data is stored in our backend database hosted on a secure cloud provider. We use industry-standard encryption in transit (TLS) and at rest. We do not store payment card numbers or banking credentials on FAWN servers.

We do not sell your data

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to any third party for marketing purposes. Ever.

Your rights

You may request deletion of your waitlist entry or account data at any time by emailing alexmarcusgoldsmith@gmail.com. We will process deletion requests within 30 days, subject to any legal retention requirements.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy as the product evolves. Material changes will be communicated by email if you have an account. The "last updated" date at the top reflects the most recent revision.
